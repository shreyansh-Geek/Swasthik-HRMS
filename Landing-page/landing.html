Our Features

Smart Queuing System (OPD)

Optimizes patient flow in OPDs through digital check-ins, real-time notifications via SMS and Emails. Data-driven queue management, reducing wait times and enhancing patient satisfaction.

Bed Management System (IPD)

Manages bed availability efficiently through real-time tracking. Automated bed assignment using AI and ML driven forecasting tools for reducing bed shortages. Displays visual bed occupancy.

Inventory Management System (Pharmacy)

Efficiently manages inventory and medicines with real-time tracking and demand forecasting. Anomaly Detection: Visualize outliers like sudden spikes in admissions or unusual inventory use. Triggers automated reordering, reducing wastage and ensuring essential supplies are always available.

Integrated City-Wide Module

Integrates these hospital systems with city-wide health modules and centralized Health information system for improved coordination.Interoperability standards for seamless real-time data sharing and enhanced response to emergency situations & critical shortages.

Patient Admission System (IPD)

Streamlines patient admission with online pre-admission. Automated documentation and verification process, patient tracking and feedback mechanism.

Patient Interaction Support & Data Visualization

AI & NLP-powered virtual assistants with 24/7 chatbot availability, voice recognition, and multilingual support. Improving patient interactions and reducing administrative workload. Maps to visualize the geographic distribution of resources, hospital locations, and real-time dashboards to visualize Stock Levels & health data.

Secure Health Records and Digital Wallets for Healthcare Payments

Blockchain systems secures patient records with decentralized, tamper-proof storage. Smart contracts for controlled access, enhancing data security and integrity. Integrates cryptocurrency with hospital billing systems for fast, secure payments using digital wallets, reducing transaction fees.
